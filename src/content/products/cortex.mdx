Developers who use Claude, Cursor, Copilot, and Gemini maintain the same rules in four incompatible formats. They drift. They contradict. They rot.

Cortex fixes the cause, not the symptom: one `.cortex/` directory, nine native configs emitted. Rules stay in sync, onboarding stops taking a week, and the "which tool is right" debate becomes "which tool does this developer prefer today."

Phones are overrun with spam, insurance pitches, and low-priority unknowns. You can't confidently ignore them — urgent calls slip through. Vexa ends that compromise.

The AI picks up. It asks who the caller is and why. It streams you a live transcript. If the call is urgent, it transfers. If not, it leaves a summary and the caller's own words, ready for you to reply in your own time.

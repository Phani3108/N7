Most expertise today is locked inside individuals — difficult to access, lost across meetings, dependent on availability. Digital Twin changes that by capturing context, behavior, and decision patterns; making expertise accessible on demand; enabling interaction, not just retrieval; and scaling knowledge beyond the individual.

Not imitation. Usable intelligence. Not storage. Interaction. Not static answers. Context-aware reasoning. Not replacement. Extension.

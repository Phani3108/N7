Most AI assistants ask you to trust a single omniscient agent with your calendar, your email, your finances, and your shopping cart. Cadbury refuses that pattern.

Seven specialized delegates, each with its own pipeline, its own policy file, its own trust zone, and its own budget. The Recruiter can auto-decline low-match roles but will never book a call without your review. The Shopping delegate watches prices but never buys. The Calendar proposes slots; you accept. Boundaries are how AI earns the right to scale.

Most task tools add process instead of clarity. Overload teams with features. Create dashboards instead of execution. Tasket fixes that by removing, not adding.

It is a lightweight execution layer, a structured task flow system, a clarity engine — for teams that already know what to do and just need a system that doesn't get in the way. Feels invisible. Keeps work moving.

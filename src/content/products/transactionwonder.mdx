SMB finance teams waste hours on invoice entry, bank reconciliation, and compliance reporting across disconnected tools. TransactionWonder replaces the tool pile with a 110-agent organisation that books, reconciles, pays, and reports.

Strict tenant isolation at the database layer. PII redaction at the executor. Circuit breakers and retry wrappers on every external call. A Stripe webhook that actually verifies the signature inside the five-minute window. Boring infrastructure is how the books stay clean.

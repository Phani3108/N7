Schools today operate on disconnected tools, manual workflows, poor visibility, and a heavy dependency on people rather than systems. Digitalization of Schools replaces that with a single operational backbone.

Not more tools. One system. From manual coordination to structured workflows. From fragmented data to unified intelligence. From admin-heavy to system-driven operations. And a foundation that's ready the day AI becomes a first-class citizen in the classroom.

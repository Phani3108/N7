Live voice calls are a performance with no director. You remember a story thirty seconds too late. You misjudge a pause. You nearly have the right number.

Shadow sits between the call and your earbud. It listens. It knows your profile. It whispers the next beat when you hesitate, the keyword when you need an anchor, or — if you invoke Shadow mode with a sealed earbud — your next full sentence in your own voice. The other person hears only you.

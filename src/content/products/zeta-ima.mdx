Marketing teams can't scale quality because brand voice, prior wins, and campaign performance stay siloed across people and tools. Zeta IMA collapses that gap.

Every asset the agency produces runs through an orchestrator with ticket routing, SLA tracking, and a critic that won't let weak copy out the door. Every campaign gets scored after launch. Every score feeds the Brain. The agency that shipped your last campaign is a smarter one today than yesterday.

## 1. What we collect

- **Account identifiers** — name, email, company, billing contact.
- **Billing data** — handled by our processor (Stripe). We store only the last four digits and expiry.
- **Agent-run logs** — prompts, tool calls, and outputs generated by the workspace you provisioned.
- **Website analytics** — aggregated, cookie-based. See [Cookie Preferences](/legal/cookies).

## 2. What we do not do

- We do **not** sell personal data.
- We do **not** train our models — or anyone else's — on Your Content.
- We do **not** share Your Content with model providers beyond what the workflow you ran requires, and we redact PII at the edge before it leaves our infrastructure.

## 3. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or erase your personal data. Write to [privacy@number7.io](mailto:privacy@number7.io). We verify requests and complete them within 30 days.

If you are a California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, or Utah resident, see [Your Privacy Choices](/legal/your-privacy-choices). If you are in the European Economic Area or India, you have additional rights under GDPR and the DPDP Act respectively.

## 4. Retention

- Agent-run logs: 13 months (tenant-configurable down to 30 days).
- Financial records: 7 years (statutory minimum in multiple jurisdictions).
- Account data: until you close the account plus 30 days for export.

## 5. Sub-processors

A current list is maintained at [/legal/subprocessors](/legal/subprocessors). We give paying customers 30 days' notice before adding a new sub-processor to the list.

## 6. Security

See [Trust](/trust) for a full posture. Highlights: SOC 2 Type II, AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, per-tenant KMS keys, and an annual third-party penetration test.

## 7. International transfers

Data residency is available in US, EU, and India regions. You choose the region on provisioning; we do not move Your Content between regions without your written instruction.

## 8. Children

Our products are not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

## 9. Changes

We will post material changes here and notify account admins by email at least 30 days before they take effect.

Questions? [privacy@number7.io](mailto:privacy@number7.io).

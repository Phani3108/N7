We use cookies and similar technologies to operate this website, remember your preferences, and — if you opt in — understand aggregated traffic patterns. We use only four categories, explained below.

## The categories, in plain English

### Strictly necessary
Required. These keep you logged in, protect against fraud, and remember what you're doing across a session. Turning them off would break the service.

### Functional
Optional, on by default. These remember preferences like region, theme, and whether you've dismissed a banner. No individual profiling.

### Analytics
Optional, **off by default**. Aggregated usage signals — how many people read a page, which link they clicked next. Never per-user profiling. We use first-party analytics only.

### Marketing
Optional, **off by default**. Tells us which campaign, ad, or referrer brought you to the site. Used only for attribution, never for retargeting.

## Managing outside the browser

You can also opt out of analytics and marketing cookies at the browser level (Do Not Track / Global Privacy Control). We honour both automatically on all browsers that send them.

## Who this page is for

Residents of **California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah**, and **India** have specific statutory rights under the CCPA/CPRA, VCDPA, CPA, CTDPA, UCPA, and DPDP Act respectively. Residents of the **European Economic Area and United Kingdom** have rights under GDPR and UK GDPR. This page explains how to exercise them with Number7.

## What we do and don't do with personal information

- We do **not** sell personal information, and we do **not** "share" it for cross-context behavioural advertising as those terms are defined in US state law.
- We do **not** use personal information for automated decisions with legal or similarly significant effects.
- We do **not** train our or anyone else's AI models on Your Content.

We still make the choices below explicit so you can exercise rights that apply to you.

## Your specific rights

### Right to know / access
Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

### Right to correct
Ask us to correct inaccurate personal information.

### Right to delete / erasure
Ask us to delete personal information, subject to statutory retention requirements (e.g., financial records held for tax purposes).

### Right to limit use of sensitive personal information
Ask us to use sensitive categories only as necessary to provide the service.

### Right to opt out of "sale" or "sharing"
Not applicable in our case — we do neither. Opting out is effective immediately because the default is already "off."

### Right to non-discrimination
You will not receive a different quality of service because you exercised any of these rights.

## How to exercise these rights

Email [privacy@number7.io](mailto:privacy@number7.io) with:

1. Your name and the account email you use with us.
2. The right you wish to exercise.
3. Evidence sufficient to verify your identity (we will request only what's necessary).

We respond within 30 days. Complex requests may be extended by a further 30 days with notice.

## Authorized agents

You may use an authorized agent to submit a request. We require written authority, a copy of the agent's government ID, and verification of your own identity. Authorized agents cannot exercise rights on behalf of more than ten individuals in a single submission.

## Appeals (VA, CO, CT, UT residents)

If we refuse a request, you may appeal by replying to our decision within 60 days. A different reviewer will respond within 60 days of the appeal, with reasoning.
